About Me

Based out of Coquitlam, BC, I’m a recent graduate of CodeCore College’s Web Developer Bootcamp, proficient in both front-end and back-end development. I am eager and in search for opportunities to expand my portfolio and apply my skills bringing elegant ideas to life.

Work Highlights

I have worked on personal projects, as well as projects in a team environment, with different levels of functionality. Ease of use and fluid design have been my main focus and I hope to expand on those skills. Below you can see some sample projects that I have worked on to get a better understanding of my work and skills.

Blood Alley Tours

Online booking site built for Blood Alley Tours, a company looking to set up walking tours in Gastown. The site has the ability for potential customers to both book and pay for a walking tour, as well as reaching out to the Blood Alley Tours team for any general inquiries.

Property Management

This Project provides a web-based solution that connects homeowners and tenants, allowing homeowners to list rental properties, and tenants to submit and track rental applications. It was developed in a team 4 as a project for the Web Developer’s Bootcamp.
